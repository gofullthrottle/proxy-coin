## 1. Introduction

Proxy Coin ("we," "us," "our") operates the Proxy Coin proxy network, PRXY token reward system, and API platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Node Operators (Android App)

| Data | Purpose | Retention |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Device ID (pseudonymous) | Node identification and rewards | Duration of account |
| IP address | Fraud detection, geo-routing | 90 days |
| Bandwidth usage metrics | Reward calculation | 2 years |
| Network type (WiFi/mobile) | Quality scoring | 90 days |
| Wallet address | On-chain reward distribution | Permanent (blockchain) |
| Android Play Integrity token | Anti-fraud attestation | 6 hours |

We do **not** collect:
- Content of traffic routed through your device
- Contact lists, photos, location (GPS), or other personal device data
- Credentials you use on websites accessed by third parties through your connection

### 2.2 API Customers

| Data | Purpose | Retention |
|------|---------|-----------|

| Password hash (bcrypt) | Authentication | Duration of account |
| API key hash | Authentication | Duration of account |
| Request URLs | Fraud detection, abuse prevention | 30 days |
| Usage metrics (bytes, requests) | Billing, reporting | 2 years |
| IP address of API caller | Security, fraud detection | 90 days |

## 3. How We Use Information

- **Reward calculation**: Computing PRXY token rewards based on bandwidth contributed
- **Fraud prevention**: Detecting fake or automated nodes that violate the Acceptable Use Policy
- **Service delivery**: Routing proxy requests to appropriate nodes
- **Billing**: Calculating API usage charges for paying customers
- **Security**: Detecting and preventing unauthorized access, abuse, and attacks
- **Legal compliance**: Meeting obligations under applicable law

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share data with:
- **Infrastructure providers**: Cloud hosting, CDN, and monitoring services under data processing agreements
- **Blockchain networks**: Wallet addresses and reward amounts are submitted on-chain (public)
- **Law enforcement**: When required by valid legal process or to protect safety
- **Successors**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

---

## 5. Blockchain Data

PRXY token claims and distributions are recorded on the Base L2 blockchain. Blockchain data is permanently public and cannot be erased. Your wallet address and cumulative reward amounts become part of the immutable public ledger when you claim rewards.

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.2+)
- Encryption of sensitive data at rest
- Password hashing with bcrypt (cost factor 12)
- API key hashing (bcrypt)
- Regular security audits

No security system is impenetrable. Please notify us immediately at security@proxycoin.io if you discover a vulnerability.

---

## 7. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy. When you delete your account:
- Account data is deleted within 30 days
- Usage metrics may be retained in anonymized/aggregated form
- Blockchain records cannot be deleted

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Objection**: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Restriction**: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@proxycoin.io

### GDPR (EU/EEA)

If you are in the EU/EEA, Proxy Coin is the data controller. Our legal bases for processing are:
- **Contract**: Providing the Service you have agreed to use
- **Legitimate interests**: Fraud prevention, security, network optimization
- **Legal obligation**: Compliance with applicable law

### CCPA (California)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of sale (we do not sell personal information).
